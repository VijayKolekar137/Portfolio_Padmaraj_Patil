SEO Optimization

SEO is the process of optimizing a website to improve its ranking on search engines by making changes to the content and code. Techniques used include keyword research, content optimization, and link building. The goal is to increase visibility and drive more traffic to the website.

Intro Videos

Intro videos are short clips, usually between 5-30 seconds, played at the beginning of a video or presentation. They introduce the content and can include elements such as a logo, text, animation, or music. They are often used to establish a brand identity and set the tone for the content.
